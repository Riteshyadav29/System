# Student Guide - QR Code Attendance System

## Overview
This attendance system allows students to mark their attendance by scanning QR codes displayed by teachers during class sessions.

## How to Use

### 1. Login
- Go to the login page and enter your student credentials
- The system will automatically detect if you're a student and show the appropriate interface

### 2. Dashboard
Once logged in, you'll see:
- **Scan QR Code**: Quick access to the QR scanner
- **My Attendance**: View your attendance history and statistics

### 3. Scanning QR Codes

#### Method 1: Camera Scanner (Recommended)
1. Click "Open QR Scanner" from the dashboard
2. Click "Start Camera Scanner" to activate your device's camera
3. Point your camera at the QR code displayed by your teacher
4. The system will automatically detect and process the QR code
5. You'll see a confirmation message with your attendance status

#### Method 2: Manual Entry
1. If camera access is not available, use the manual entry option
2. Ask your teacher for the QR token
3. Enter the token in the "Manual Token Entry" field
4. Click "Submit Token"

### 4. Attendance Status
- **Present**: Marked within the first 10 minutes of class
- **Late**: Marked between 10-20 minutes of class start
- **Absent**: After 20 minutes, the attendance window closes

### 5. Viewing Attendance History
1. Click "My Attendance" from the dashboard
2. View your attendance statistics and recent records
3. See today's scheduled classes
4. Track your attendance percentage

## Features

### Mobile-Friendly Design
- Optimized for mobile devices
- Camera automatically uses the back camera on mobile
- Touch-friendly interface

### Real-time Feedback
- Instant confirmation when attendance is marked
- Clear error messages if scanning fails
- Toast notifications for success/failure

### Security
- QR codes change every 5 seconds
- Tokens expire automatically
- One-time use tokens prevent duplicate marking

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Issues
- **Permission Denied**: Allow camera access in your browser settings
- **No Camera Found**: Use the manual entry method instead
- **Poor Quality**: Ensure good lighting and hold the device steady

### Network Issues
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if problems persist

### QR Code Issues
- Make sure the QR code is clearly visible
- Try adjusting the distance from the screen
- Ask the teacher to refresh the QR code if it's not working

## Best Practices

1. **Arrive on Time**: Scan within the first 10 minutes for "Present" status
2. **Good Lighting**: Ensure adequate lighting when scanning
3. **Steady Hands**: Hold your device steady while scanning
4. **Backup Method**: Know how to use manual entry as a backup
5. **Check Results**: Always verify that your attendance was marked successfully

## Support

If you encounter any issues:
1. Try the troubleshooting steps above
2. Use the manual entry method as a backup
3. Contact your teacher or system administrator
4. Check the system status page for any ongoing issues

## Privacy

- Your attendance data is securely stored
- Only authorized personnel can view your records
- The system logs when and how attendance was marked
- Your personal information is protected according to privacy policies
